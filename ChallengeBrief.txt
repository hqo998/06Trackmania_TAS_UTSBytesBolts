The Time Attack challenge takes place within the Trackmania Nations Forever game engine. Teams are required to achieve high-speed times in two distinct environments: a "seen" course available from the start of the challenge, and a "custom/unseen" course that is only revealed during the testing period.

Operational Requirements
Teams must use the provided online platform to publish lap times to the leaderboard. The competition involves both human-driven times and potential applications of machine learning (ML) or Artificial Intelligence (AI) to optimize racing lines.

Criteria and Outcomes
The scoring system for this challenge rewards both baseline completion and high-level performance metrics, including competitive rankings.

Points	Challenge	Level
20	Complete "seen" course and publish time.	Base
5	Complete "seen" course with NO human input (e.g., AI/ML).	Easy/Moderate
15	Complete "seen" course with NO human input AND beat the judge's time.	Moderate
10-0	Top 10 teams on the leaderboard for the "seen" course.	Hard
20	Complete a full lap of the "unseen" custom course.	Moderate/Hard
15	Complete "unseen" course and beat the development team's time.	Hard
10-0	Top 10 teams on the leaderboard for the "unseen" course.	Hard
5	Set a real-world speed record (Top 10) in any tool-assisted category.	Hard
